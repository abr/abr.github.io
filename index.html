Oh, you want to know what we actually do?

At ABR we are developing a new kind of AI that exploits spiking in neural networks. This means both the algorithms and the hardware we use ranges from the conventional (CPUs, GPUs, deep networks, ANNs) to the unconventional (spiking ASICs, FPGAs, adaptive controllers, SNNs). We have deep expertise in using neuromorphic (spiking) hardware and software to solve AI problems. We have developed a software platform, Nengo, that let's you combine methods that get you the lowest power, fastest speeds, and greatest accuracy.
